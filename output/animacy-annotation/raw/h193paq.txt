Replaying Mass Effect trilogy for the 6th time and I can’t help but think of how amazing it would’ve been for Cyberpunk 2077 to have a hub space like The Citadel DLC, where you invite your crew members, party, hangout, do quests with each of them or chat and know them better.

Cyberpunk 2077 has this incredible world and lore and it could use something to encourage more activities and things to do to evolve the bonds you make with people, all of whom feel rather completely disconnected and unaware of each other.
