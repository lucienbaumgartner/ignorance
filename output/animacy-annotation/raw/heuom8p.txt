They can't lock them up because, as the article says, "With four or five people, they were able to sedate the patient, who was severely ill and unaware of his actions."

People in hospitals behave unpredictably for a lot of reasons. Sometimes, there is malice and willful behavior, but sometimes they're confused because of their illness or medication. Proving they are in a state in which they are in control of their actions would be very difficult. This only applies to patients, however, not to their family who visit.
